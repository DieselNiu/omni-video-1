## Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Gemini Omni uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit the website, sign in, generate images, or manage your account.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies We Use

We currently use a limited set of cookies and browser storage for product operation:

- **Essential cookies** to keep the site working, maintain authenticated sessions, protect account access, support checkout flows, and help enforce guest usage limits and anti-abuse controls.
- **Preference cookies** to remember settings such as your language, active theme, and interface state.
- **Operational storage** such as local storage or temporary browser state to support login flows, dialogs, and similar product interactions.

## Examples in This Product

Depending on how you use the service, these technologies may be used to:

- keep you signed in and remember secure session state;
- remember locale and UI preferences;
- preserve guest access and free-generation quota state;
- reduce fraud, abuse, repeated submissions, and service misuse;
- keep payment or billing flows working correctly.

## What We Do Not Use as Core Product Behavior

The core product is not built around advertising cookies or third-party ad targeting. If we later enable additional analytics, experiments, or marketing tags, we will update this policy.

## Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you delete or block cookies, or alert you before a cookie is set. You can also clear local storage in your browser settings.

If you block essential cookies or similar storage, parts of Gemini Omni may not work correctly. For example, login, language settings, guest usage limits, checkout, or saved interface preferences may stop working.

## Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page.
