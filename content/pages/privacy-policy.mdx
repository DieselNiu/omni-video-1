## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Gemini Omni collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit the website, create an account, generate or edit images, purchase credits or subscriptions, or contact us.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- **Account information**: your name, email address, profile image, and authentication provider details when you sign in with Google or another supported provider.
- **Billing information**: plan, credit, subscription, purchase history, billing status, and payment-processor identifiers returned by providers such as Stripe. We do not store full payment card numbers.
- **Content you provide**: prompts, uploaded images, reference files, generation settings, support messages, and outputs that you save in the product.
- **Usage and technical information**: browser type, device information, approximate network information, locale, timestamps, pages used, feature interactions, logs, and error diagnostics.
- **Security and anti-abuse data**: guest identifiers, quota state, request metadata, and hashed or derived signals used to prevent fraud, abuse, and repeated misuse of the service.

## How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

- provide, operate, and maintain Gemini Omni;
- authenticate users and protect account access;
- process image generation, editing, storage, and downloads;
- manage credits, subscriptions, checkout, refunds, and billing records;
- respond to support requests and service communications;
- monitor reliability, debug issues, and improve product performance;
- detect abuse, enforce limits, and protect the service, our users, and third parties;
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## How We Share Information

We share information only when reasonably necessary to run the product, including with:

- authentication providers;
- payment processors such as Stripe;
- email and support service providers;
- cloud hosting and S3-compatible storage providers;
- AI model and inference providers that process prompts, uploads, and generation requests on our behalf;
- legal authorities or counterparties when disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal information.

## Data Retention

We keep information for as long as it is needed to operate the service, maintain your account, provide purchased features, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and satisfy legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data, account status, and security needs.

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You can also manage some information directly inside your account, delete assets you no longer want stored, or stop using the service at any time.

If you choose not to provide certain information, some features may not work.

## International Processing

Our service providers may process information in countries other than your own. By using the service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection rules.

## Children's Privacy

Gemini Omni is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required by local law. Do not use the service if you are not old enough to do so legally.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise applicable privacy rights, please [contact us](/contact).
